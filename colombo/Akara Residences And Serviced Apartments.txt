About Amagi Aria - Negombo's Hidden Secret
Set adjacent to the Negombo Lagoon, Amagi Aria - Negombo's Hidden Secret offers luxury accommodation close to Sri Lanka’s Bandaranaike International Airport.

Amagi Aria - Negombo's Hidden Secret offers a mix of standard, superior and deluxe rooms. Standard rooms are ideal for couples while deluxe rooms can accommodate a maximum of four people. All rooms feature Wi-Fi, tea and coffee facilities, flat-screen TVs and wardrobes.

In addition to a gym and swimming pool, this resort’s spa offers a range of holistic services.

24-hour room service is available at this resort. However, guests can enjoy a meal in the Lagoon, the property’s main restaurant, or unwind with a drink at one of two on-site bars, the Catamaran and Tree House.

This hotel is located just north of Colombo and is connected to the sights and sounds of Sri Lanka’s capital city by the Chilaw-Colombo Main Road, which runs adjacent to the property.
