About Cinnamon Red Colombo
Located in Central Colombo, Sri Lanka, Cinnamon Red Colombo is just a ten-minute walk from the Liberty Cinema Hall. This contemporary hotel takes pride in its rooftop infinity swimming pool with a vibrant bar, offering scenic skyline views of the city.

All rooms are carpeted and fully air-conditioned. Each is equipped with a wardrobe, flat-screen TV, separate seating area, and coffee and tea making facilities.

Currency exchange and laundry services are available from the 24-hour front desk. There is also a fitness centre for working out and exercising. Moreover, both Wi-Fi and parking are offered on-site.

The hotel houses the city’s only rooftop bar - Cloud Red. Guests may also dine in Stirred, the lobby restaurant, or in Flavoured, which serves international cuisine.

Cinnamon Red Colombo is just less than a mile from Sri Lanka National Museum. The Bandaranaike International Airport can be reached through an hour drive.
