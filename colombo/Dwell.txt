About Dwell
Set in Colombo, 90 metres from U.S. Embassy, dwell features a fitness centre. This 4-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk. Guests can chill out in the outdoor swimming pool. The units in the hotel are equipped with a flat-screen TV. With a private bathroom fitted with free toiletries, rooms at dwell also have free WiFi, while selected rooms also feature a sea view. All rooms at the accommodation have air conditioning and a desk. A buffet breakfast is available each morning at the property. Khan Clock Tower is 2.7 km from dwell. The nearest airport is Bandaranaike International Airport, 29 km from the hotel.
