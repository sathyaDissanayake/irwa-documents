About Cinnamon Grand Colombo
Cinnamon Grand Colombo is a beautiful five star hotel in Sri Lanka's capital city. It offers something for everyone, whether travelling for business or pleasure.

All rooms offer the basic amenities including air-conditioning, TV and Internet, 24 hour room service and others. Some of the more special features are a choice from the pillow menu, a welcome bath and complimentary newspaper.

Cinnamon Grand Colombo offers a whole host of facilities to every guest, including the Angsana Spa, business centre, Crescat shopping centre, gym, children's playroom, swimming pool and a number of lounges and relaxation areas.

The hotel offers over a dozen restaurants and bars for any occasion and catering to every need.

Cinnamon Grand is a short drive away from the airport and within easy access of Colombo's vibrant city centre.
