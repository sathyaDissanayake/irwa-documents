About Heina Villa Colombo Sri Lanka
Enjoy Nature, sooth your tired mind and relax in a typical village environment

Akin to a phoenix rising from the ashes, Heina brings a truly one-with-nature concept to a more refined and elegant form with Heina Villa, set within the immediate outskirts of the busy Colombo city, boasts a tremendous commitment to sustainability and the environment. Heina Villa offers a typical village experience complemented with the finest in luxury and comfort in an antique style. 
Heina villa situated in an artistic location in salubrious conditions on a picturesque hill overlooking Diyawannawa lake and Sri Jayawardenapura bird sanctuary, conveniently located just outside the busy city, away from the noise, is a perfect place to unwind and relax. Natural beauty of the surround will help to sooth your tired mind and relax in a typical village environment, away from the hustle and bustle of the city.
It is only 4 km from the parliament, 7 km from National Museum, 7 km from Colombo city center and 29 km from Bandaranaike International Airport.
Here the dawn is greeted by chirping melody of birds and nights lit by garden lanterns, all adding to the delightful experience in a village setting.
