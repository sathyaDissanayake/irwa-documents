About Ramada Colombo
Conveniently located in the center of Colombo, this familiar brand lives up to its reputation for excellent service and value for money. A five-minute walk from the train station, Galle Face Green and the ramparts of the beachfront, the Holiday Inn Colombo is in a superlative location, with three-wheelers and taxis available just outside the hotel. A short drive away, guests can access the National Museum, Pettah Bazaar and the Lighthouse with minimal fuss, while myriad restaurants, bars and shops are accessible on foot. Rooms are welcoming and smartly decorated, providing a comfortable retreat in which to recharge after a day coursing Colombo s busy streets.
