About Taj Samudra
Featuring unforgettable, exquisite interiors, as well as an outdoor pool , Taj Samudra is found just a five-minute walk from Galle Face Beach.

Boasting modern furnishings throughout, each room offers king or queen-size beds, tea and coffee-making facilities, and a marble en-suite bathroom. Suites at Taj Samudra feature spacious living rooms, some of which include floor-to-ceiling windows.

Surrounded by lush gardens, tennis courts, and outdoor terraces, Taj Samudra exudes elegance. There is also a gym on site, and a range of meeting and conference facilities. Children will also be kept entertained at this hotel with a range of creative activities.

Ports of Call Restaurant hosts on-site breakfast each morning, while Golden Dragon serves Chinese dishes for lunch and dinner. Indian foods are available at Navratna, also located on site.

A five-kilometre drive from this hotel will put guests on the steps of National Museum, and a nine-minute walk will take guests to Kompannavidiya Railway Station. Excel World Entertainment Park is three kilometres away.
