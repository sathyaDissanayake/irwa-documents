About Colombo Court
The Colombo Court is a 32-room, eco-friendly hotel with an airport shuttle service to nearby Colombo International Airport.

Each room is air-conditioned and features a large-screen LED TV, daily complimentary bottled water, welcome fruit basket, mini-bar, tea and coffee making facility, free Wi-Fi, hair dryer, iron and ironing board, in-room safe, a private bathroom and indoor rain shower.

The Colombo Court boasts an outdoor pool, sun terrace, garden as well as a fitness centre, spa, massage parlour and library. Free Wi-Fi is available in all public areas and free parking on-site is available by reservation.

A rooftop café is onsite and provides sweeping views of the city.  The Scarlett restaurant offers European, Mediterranean and Asian food, and the Loft Lounge Bar offers a selection of fine wines.

The property offers quick access to the city centre, the Kopity Railway Station and MC Shopping Mall are nearby.
