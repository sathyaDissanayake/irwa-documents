About Anilana Nilaveli
This lovely beach front accommodation is nestled in lush green gardens in Trincomalee, and features complimentary Wi-Fi throughout the property.

The elegantly furnished, and air-conditioned guest rooms at Anilana Nilaveli features complimentary vanities, a bathrobe and slippers, while a flat screen TV and private terrace is standard.

Amenities at this hotel include complimentary parking for guests, while there is also a luggage storage facility and an on-site laundry service. Fishing and canoeing are offered nearby.

The on-site restaurant at Anilana Nilaveli serves a variety of local dishes, while hotel staff will prepare any special meal requests. Cuisine is prepared from locally grown produce.

Guests can explore the ancient capitals of Sri Lanka, within a 20 minute drive, or visit the hot water wells in Kinniya, as well as the Pigeon Island which is fifteen minutes by car.
